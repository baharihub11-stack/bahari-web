BAHARI NETWORK

Decentralized Participation Protocol

1. Executive Summary

Bahari Network is a mobile-first digital asset distribution system designed to lower the barrier of entry into blockchain participation. Unlike traditional mining systems that require expensive hardware, Bahari introduces a Proof-of-Activity model, where users earn rewards through verified daily engagement.

The goal is to build a large, verified user base before transitioning to a fully functional on-chain ecosystem where BHR acts as a utility token.

2. Token Overview (BHR)

Maximum Supply: 210,000,000 BHR (Fixed)

  • Community Distribution: 78%
  • Development Reserve: 10%
  • Ecosystem & Utility: 12%

All allocations are locked and released gradually under a 24-month vesting schedule to prevent supply shocks.

3. Earning Model (Proof of Activity)

Users earn BHR through daily verified participation inside the Bahari application.

Current Base Rate: 2.60 BHR / 24 hours
After Halving: 1.30 BHR / 24 hours

The system uses a Halving Mechanism where rewards decrease by 50% as the network grows.

This ensures:

  • Early adopters are rewarded
  • Token scarcity increases over time
  • Total supply is controlled

Reward Formula:

Daily Reward = Base Rate × (1/2)n

Where:
n = number of halving events

4. Anti-Abuse & Verification

To maintain fairness, Bahari Network implements multiple verification layers:

  • Device-level validation
  • Behavioral activity checks
  • Future KYC verification (Phase II)

These systems ensure that rewards go only to real users, preventing bot farming and exploitation.

5. Sustainability Model

Bahari introduces a revenue-backed ecosystem to support long-term stability.

  • Ad-based revenue (non-intrusive)
  • Premium in-app features
  • Task & quest marketplace

30% of net revenue is allocated to:

  • Liquidity support
  • Market buyback
  • Token burn events

All buyback operations will be publicly trackable once on-chain.

6. Utility of BHR
