BAHARI STRATEGIC BLUEPRINT

Version 1.0 | Last Updated: April 2026

1. THE VISION

Bahari Network is a decentralized ecosystem built on the principle of Absolute Scarcity. With a hard cap of 210 Million tokens, we aim to bridge the gap between mobile accessibility and real-world value.

2. ASSET SCARCITY (TOKENOMICS)

  • Total Max Supply: 210,000,000 BHR
  • Distribution: 70% Community Mining, 15% Ecosystem, 10% Team, 5% Rewards.
  • Halving Mechanism: To protect value, mining rates are reduced by 50% as the community grows. Early pioneers receive the highest rewards.

3. SUSTAINABILITY (THE REVENUE LOOP)

Unlike speculative projects, Bahari is backed by a Buyback Strategy. Revenue generated through in-app interactions is used to fund future liquidity pools, ensuring the BHR token has real-world support from Day 1.

4. THE ROADMAP

Phase 1: Distribution (Current)
Focus: Global community growth and fair token distribution through mobile mining.

Phase 2: Validation (KYC & Utility)
Trigger: 500,000 Pioneers.
Focus: Identity verification, anti-bot measures, and the launch of the "Bahari Quest" utility platform.

Phase 3: Liquidity (Mainnet & Exchange)
Trigger: 1,000,000 Pioneers.
Focus: Token migration to blockchain and listing on Decentralized (DEX) and Centralized (CEX) exchanges.
