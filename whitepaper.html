BAHARI NETWORK

Sustainable Mobile-First Digital Asset Protocol

1. Overview

Bahari Network is a mobile-first digital asset platform designed to make participation in crypto ecosystems accessible to everyone.

Users earn BHR through simple daily verified activity without requiring expensive hardware or upfront investment.

The system begins as an off-chain reward infrastructure for scalability and will transition to blockchain-based settlement in later phases.

2. Token (BHR)

Maximum Supply: 210,000,000 BHR

  • Community Distribution: 78%
  • Development Reserve: 10%
  • Ecosystem Growth: 12%

All allocations follow a controlled vesting schedule over 24 months to maintain supply stability and prevent sudden market shocks.

3. Earning Model

Users earn BHR through verified daily participation within the Bahari application.

Current Base Rate: 2.60 BHR / 24 hours
Post-Halving Rate: 1.30 BHR / 24 hours

Reward Formula:

Reward = Base Rate × (1/2)n

Where n represents the number of halving events.

This model ensures controlled issuance, increasing scarcity over time, and fair distribution among early and late participants.

4. Sustainability Model (Why Ads Exist)

Bahari Network uses a light, non-intrusive advertising model (e.g., AdMob) to fund the ecosystem without requiring users to pay fees or make deposits.

Revenue generated from ads is allocated across three core areas:

  • Infrastructure: server costs, app maintenance, and system reliability
  • Growth: user acquisition, marketing, and ecosystem expansion
  • Token Economy: liquidity support, buybacks, and controlled burn mechanisms

This approach allows users to participate freely while supporting long-term sustainability and economic stability of the network.

5. System Architecture

Phase I: Off-chain reward system for scalability and rapid adoption.

Phase II: Introduction of identity verification and non-custodial wallet infrastructure.

Phase III: Blockchain integration, token transferability, and exchange readiness.
