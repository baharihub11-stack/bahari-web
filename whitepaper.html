01. The Global Vision

Bahari Network is a decentralized architecture engineered to facilitate Network Sovereignty. We reject the high-barrier entry models of traditional digital mining. By implementing a mobile-first Protocol of Presence, Bahari empowers a global community of Pioneers to participate in a high-scarcity ecosystem with zero hardware friction.

03. Strategic Roadmap

PHASE I: GENESIS & DISTRIBUTION

Global deployment of the Bahari interface. Focus on viral network expansion and high-velocity community acquisition. Verification of baseline ledger stability.

PHASE II: INTEGRITY & UTILITY

Implementation of the Authentication Protocol (KYC). Integration of non-intrusive revenue models to scale infrastructure. Deployment of the Bahari non-custodial wallet beta.

PHASE III: EXCHANGE & MAINNET

Finalization of technical standards for CEX and DEX exchange listings. Transition of verified balances to the permanent Mainnet. Expansion into a cross-border utility marketplace.
