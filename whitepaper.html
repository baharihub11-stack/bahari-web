BAHARI NETWORK

Sustainable Mobile-First Digital Asset Protocol

1. Overview

Bahari Network is a mobile-first digital asset platform designed to make participation in crypto ecosystems accessible to everyone.

Users earn BHR through simple daily activity without requiring expensive hardware or upfront investment.

The system begins off-chain for scalability and will transition to blockchain infrastructure in later phases.

2. Token (BHR)

Maximum Supply: 210,000,000 BHR

  • Community Distribution: 78%
  • Development: 10%
  • Ecosystem Growth: 12%

Token release follows a controlled vesting schedule to maintain long-term stability.

3. Earning Model

Users earn BHR through verified daily participation inside the app.

Current Rate: 2.60 BHR / 24 hours
After Halving: 1.30 BHR / 24 hours

Reward Formula:

Reward = Base × (1/2)n

This halving system ensures decreasing issuance, protects supply, and rewards early adopters.

4. Why Ads Exist (Sustainability Model)

Bahari Network uses a light, non-intrusive advertising model (such as AdMob) to fund the ecosystem.

Instead of charging users fees or requiring deposits, ads allow the platform to remain free and accessible.

This creates a fair system where:

  • Users earn without financial risk
  • The platform generates real revenue
  • The ecosystem can sustain long-term growth

30% of net revenue is allocated to:

  • Liquidity support
  • Token buybacks
  • Supply reduction (burn events)

Ads are designed to be minimal and never interrupt core user experience.

5. System Architecture

Phase I operates as an off-chain reward system for speed and scalability.

Phase II introduces identity verification and wallet infrastructure.

Phase III enables blockchain integration and token transfers.
